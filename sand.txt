The quantity of ingredients needed for a Sandwich is:
1 potato large
1 green  chilli chopped
1/2 teaspoon red chilli powder
1/2 garam masal powder 
1 tbsp lemon juice
1 tbsp coriander leaves
2 slices white bread
2 tbsp butter (toasting)
1/4 green chutney

Coffee:
3 tbsp Nestle coffee grains
100ml full cream milk		
2 tbsp sugar (as per requirement)
3-4 drops Hershey's chocolate syrup
Sprinkle chocolate powder
